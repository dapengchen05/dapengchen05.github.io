About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Lehigh University. My research lies at the intersection of health and labor economics, examining how institutional environments and policy reforms shape decision-making by healthcare providers and families, and how these behavioral responses influence healthcare quality, population health, and overall well-being.
